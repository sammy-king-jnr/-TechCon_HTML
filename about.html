Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small regional meetup of passionate developers, engineers, and tech thinkers. Over the years, it has grown into an international conference known for inspiring innovation and building community.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to connect minds and spark ideas that shape the future of technology. We are committed to fostering a diverse, inclusive, and forward-thinking tech community.

We aim to empower individuals and organizations to learn, share, and lead in a rapidly evolving digital world.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Maya Lin

A pioneer in AI ethics, Dr. Lin’s keynote in 2021 inspired a wave of innovation in responsible AI design.

Jamal Bryant

Jamal is known for revolutionizing cloud architecture and has led efforts in sustainable tech infrastructure. His 2022 session was a crowd favorite.

Sofia Alvarez

Sofia’s groundbreaking work in quantum computing was featured in 2023, delivering insight into the future of computing power.
